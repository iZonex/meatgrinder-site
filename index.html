Every tool earned its place

If a feature didn't improve results in backtesting and forward testing, we removed it. What's left is what actually works.

Volume Profile

Multi-session institutional support and resistance levels. POC migration tracking, cluster detection, range sweep identification. Validated across 90+ days of live data on BTC, ETH, and SOL.

Order Flow

Real-time absorption detection, initiative tracking, exhaustion signals, book sweeps, and VWAP bands. Not lagging indicators — actual live market microstructure data.

Smart Money Detection

Whale activity tracking, large block trade alerts, and market manipulation pattern recognition. Identifies institutional accumulation and distribution before it shows on the chart.

Liquidation Heatmap

Three-phase liquidation analysis: magnet zones that attract price, sweep detection when fuel is consumed, and cascade absorption when whales step in. Context, not just data points.

Real-Time Scanner

Every Bybit perpetual future, live. Filter by volume, momentum, funding rate, hot score, or gainers. Find the pairs worth analyzing in seconds, not minutes.

100% On-Device

Rust core compiled natively. Zero cloud dependencies. API keys in iOS Keychain. No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry. We literally cannot see your data or your trades.
