Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

MeatGrinder ("the App") is a crypto futures analysis terminal developed by Dmytro Chystiakov. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. The App has no backend servers, no analytics, and no tracking.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • API Keys — Your Bybit API credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain (hardware-encrypted secure storage). They are used solely to communicate directly with the Bybit exchange API.
  • App Settings — Your preferences (display settings, risk parameters) are stored locally using standard iOS storage.

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Network Communication

The App communicates directly with:

  • Bybit API (api.bybit.com) — to fetch market data, account information, and execute trades on your behalf using your API keys.
  • Binance API (fapi.binance.com) — to fetch supplementary market data (klines with taker volume) for analysis purposes only. No authentication or personal data is sent.
  • Alternative.me API — to fetch the Fear & Greed Index. No personal data is sent.

All network communication uses encrypted HTTPS/WSS connections. No data is routed through our servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. There are no cookies, no fingerprinting, and no user profiling.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties. We have no access to your data.

Data Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. To delete all App data:

  • Delete the App from your device — this removes all stored settings and Keychain entries.
  • Alternatively, use the "Disconnect" option in Settings to remove your API keys.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact
